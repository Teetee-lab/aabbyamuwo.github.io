About Me

I am Titilayo Amuwo, you can call me abby. I specialize in the fields of Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cloud computing solution. I received my Associate degree from Rufus Giwa Polytechnic in 2008 and Bachelors from the University of Lagos in 2015, where I studied business administration and management. After my associate degree, I landed my first job as a security trading operation specialist at Stanbic IBTC Bank. After working as a banker, I continued to work in the field of accounting and finance; that means I not only have experience as an operation specialist but also in accounting where I get to analyze data and share reports with stakeholder.
With that being said, since my teenage years, I have always had a passion for technology and innovation; aside from that, I am a go-to person among family and friends when it comes to information technology, so this has always been my passion; however, things change along the line and I have to follow the family tradition of studying business and management. While working as an accountant, I realized that we use data every day and the same data is used to build a dashboard where we can share valuable information with our clients about their finances. I wanted to learn more about the designs and software, and use it to develop my skill set; hence, I decided to merge my previous experience using data with my passion for technology and innovation by pursuing a career in information technology.
Since transitioning into a data science career, I have been contracted to work at companies and also had the opportunity to intern at one of the leading cybersecurity company in Palo Alto. All things considered, I am a versatile Data science professional with experience in compiling, transforming, and analyzing complex information using data science skills. In addition to that, as a data scientist, I have helped developed advance visualization concepts and implemented various machine learning algorithms that allow businesses to make better decisions which yield high returns on investment. My background experience allows me to provide insights that drive better business decisions and I am confident in my ability to use technical and interpersonal skills to make an impact in any organization.
Outside of work, I enjoy hanging out with family and friends, drawing, reading, and volunteering.

Landmark Tagging for Social Media

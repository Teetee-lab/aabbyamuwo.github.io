About Me

I am Titilayo Amuwo, you can call me Abby. I am a dedicated and results-driven professional who has recently transitioned into the dynamic field of data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, leveraging my comprehensive experience in business and accounting to bring a unique perspective to the world of data and technology.
With a background in business administration and management, I have honed my ability to understand the intricate workings of organizations, their operations, and the challenges they face in the financial market. My years of working in accounting have given me a solid foundation in statistical analysis, quantitative analysis and attention to detail, which now apply to dissecting complex data sets.
My journey into data science has allowed me to combine my financial awareness and strategic thinking with cutting edge data analysis and machine learning techniques and I find immense satisfaction in uncovering insights from data, by transforming raw information into actionable strategies, and ultimately driving informed decision-making.
Also, my passion for data science and technology extends beyond the numbers. I am committed to continuous learning, staying current with the latest tools and methodologies in this ever-evolving field. Aside from that, I thrive in collaborative environments, where my ability to communicate technical findings to non-technical stakeholders bridges the gap between data and real-world application.
In this new chapter of my career, I am excited to make use of my diverse skills to tackle complex data challenges, drive innovation, and make a meaningful impact. As a data scientist with a strong foundation in business and finance, I bring a holistic perspective to problem-solving and a commitment to excellence in every data-driven endeavor.
With that being said, I look forward to opportunities that will allow me to combine my analytical and statistical expertise with my business insights, contributing to the success of organizations and projects while continuing to learn and grow in this exciting field.
